About Us

Foursquare Gospel Church is a Christ-centered, Bible-believing church with a passion for evangelism, discipleship, and the move of the Holy Spirit. We believe Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever. Our mission is to raise spiritually vibrant Christians who will impact their families, communities, and the world for Christ.

Founded on the four-fold gospel—Jesus Christ as the Savior, Baptizer with the Holy Spirit, Healer, and Coming King—we stand as a beacon of light in our generation. Through sound teaching, powerful worship, and community outreach, we are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission.

Our Mission

To communicate the life-changing power of Jesus Christ to every soul, nurturing them in faith and equipping them for service in God's Kingdom.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered, Spirit-filled, and purpose-driven church raising generations that will reflect God's glory in every sphere of life.
